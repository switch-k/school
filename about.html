ABOUT US

ICBMD is an institute of management development, incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters ACT 1990, with registration number ®1634427.

The institution has the required licence to train, innovate and develop within and outside nigeria on Project Management Professional (PMP), Human Resources Management Professional (HRMP), Health Safety and Environment (HSE), Organisation Management Professional (OMP), International Customers Service and Relastionship Management (ICSRM), Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA), Digital Marketing (DM), Consultancy service ¨.

Our aim is to help organisations, Ministries, Agencies and individuals suceed by delivering internationally accredited and relevant management and health safety training, certification services that are recognised by national and international bodies.

Over the years, we have been awarded internationally and nationally as one of the top leading management institute as endorsed by the Nigeria government through federal ministry of education, center for management development(CMD),PMI,NEBOSH,OSHA.

More so, Our professionals possess both industrial and consultative expertise to effectively partner with our clients to solve their complex business, socio-economic challenges and deliver result. with high track record of excellence in service delivery, we offer multiple learning opportunities viz classroom training, field inspection, online training. in addition, we offer job search assistance and free career consultancy services after training.
